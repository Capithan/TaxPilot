Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Information We Collect

The Tax Intake API collects tax-related information that you provide during the intake process, including:

  • Personal information (name, contact details)
  • Filing status and dependent information
  • Income sources and types
  • Deduction and credit information
  • Document collection status

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Preparing for your tax appointment
  • Generating personalized document checklists
  • Matching you with an appropriate tax professional
  • Estimating appointment duration
  • Sending appointment reminders

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With your assigned tax professional for appointment preparation
  • When required by law

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Data Retention

Your information is retained only for the duration necessary to complete tax preparation services and as required by tax regulations.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact your tax professional directly.
